About Me

Afan Ali Khan 🚀

I am a passionate young developer from Chitral, Pakistan, focused on building real-world digital solutions through code.

I started my journey at the age of 11, learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and quickly moved into building full projects including websites, games, and backend systems.

My biggest project is DUR KHOJ, a complete real estate platform where users can post, browse, and manage properties using Firebase and interactive maps.

I am also the founder of Global Student Bridge, an initiative focused on connecting students and creating opportunities for learning and growth.

Alongside that, I am working on Chitral Pride Vision, a project aimed at promoting my region, culture, and local talent through technology.

My skillset includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Firebase, and Game Development. I also use tools like Git, GitHub, and VS Code in my daily workflow.

Currently, I am exploring C language and low-level programming to deepen my understanding of how systems work internally.

My goal is to become a world-class developer and use technology to solve real problems and create impactful platforms.
